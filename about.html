Zhanat Orazbayev was born in Kazakhstan, in the Zhetysu region.

The Zhetysu region is a paradise on our planet, rich in the diversity of it’s nature.

Even at a young boy, Zhanat often traveled with his parents and admired the beauty of the nature of the Zhetysu region.

After, Zhanat began to admire female beauty.

He loves everything beautiful, real and natural.

In works he combine the female beauty and the beauty of nature.

All photos was creating in natural light. Didn't use retouch and flash.

He have been doing photography since 2020.
